Moody’s Analytics

Moody’s Analytics is a part of Moody’s Corporation. The company provides financial intelligence and analytical tools to help business leaders make better, faster decisions. Their in-depth risk expertise, expansive information resources, and innovative application of technology help clients confidently navigate an evolving marketplace. Moody’s Analytics are known for their industry-leading and award-winning solutions, made up of research, data, software, and professional services, assembled to deliver a seamless customer experience.

As financial risk and regulation grow increasingly complex, Moody’s Analytics looks forward to continuing to help risk professionals make informed decisions and build successful strategies.
